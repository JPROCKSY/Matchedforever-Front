Why we're here

MatchedForever was founded by a mother-daughter duo who understand the preferences of both generations. They knew there was a need for a one-stop solution for educated professionals and business leaders seeking serious, marriage-oriented relationships, with the added support of offline assistance.

Crafting Lifelong Matches with Purpose and Precision

Every client joining our community receives a personalized call from the MatchedForever team. We provide highly exclusive and discreet services for High Net Worth Individuals (HNIs) seeking a private, elite experience, as well as flexible, transparent plans for educated professionals.

During the call, our experts ask insightful questions to understand your unique needs and preferences, address any concerns, and recommend the ideal plan to align with your personality, goals, and aspirations.

A Seamless Journey to Finding Your
Match

Begin your matchmaking journey with ease and confidence. Our streamlined process is designed to quickly and effortlessly guide you from application to membership, ensuring a personalized and smooth experience every step of the way.

1

Application Submission

Complete our comprehensive application form to share your preferences, background, and relationship goals. Our exclusive pre-screening questionnaire and interview process not only help us understand your needs but also provide you with valuable insights into what you're truly seeking in a partner, ensuring a personalised and effective matchmaking experience.

2

Consultation & Verification

Once we receive your application, you can schedule a consultation call, or our team will reach out to guide you through the next steps. During this call, we verify your details, understand your specific criteria, and assist you in choosing the ideal plan for your needs. If you desire extreme exclusivity and privacy, we offer offline plans with direct support from a dedicated relationship manager. For greater convenience, transparency, and more options, our online plans are a perfect fit. Additionally, our hybrid plans combine the benefits of both, offering flexibility and privacy. We're happy to address any questions you may have along the way.

3

Select Your Plan & Become a Member

After selecting the plan that best suits your requirements, you’ll officially become a member of MatchedForever. From here, you can begin your partner search directly through our platform. For our elite clients, our dedicated team takes over, working closely with you to find and present matches that meet your unique preferences and expectations.
